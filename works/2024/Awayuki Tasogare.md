What is death for the lifeless, and what is life for the deathless?  
Matsushita Yuki, a cheerful girl meeting her untimely demise. Nagumo Yuu, a recluse with an unknown past and an even more uncertain future.  
The Reaper, who had long forgotten his name. Under the dusty sky of this forlorn town, their paths crossed.
